Koolaid Tutorial

How to stir things up.

Home

Ingredients

  • 3 packages of your favorite flavor of koolaid
  • 1.5 cups of sugar
  • 1 gallon of cold water

Steps

  1. In a LARGE juice pitcher (1 gallon), stir together the 3 packages of koolaid and the sugar.
  2. Add about 1/3 of the water and stir well, making sure Kool-Aid and sugar dissolves.
  3. Slowly stir in remaining water; chill.
  4. Enjoy!
